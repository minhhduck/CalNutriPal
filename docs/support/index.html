Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a meal plan?

To create a meal plan in CalNutriPal:

  1. Go to the Food Log tab
  2. Tap the "+" button to add a meal
  3. Choose meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack)
  4. Add food items from our database or create custom items
  5. Save your meal

You can view your daily nutrition totals on the Dashboard.

How do I set nutrition goals?

To set custom nutrition goals:

  1. Go to the Profile tab
  2. Scroll to the Nutrition Goals section
  3. Tap the edit icon next to any goal
  4. Enter your desired values for calories, proteins, carbs, and fats
  5. Save your changes

The app will automatically track your progress toward these goals.

How do I update my body stats?

To update your body stats:

  1. Go to the Profile tab
  2. Scroll to the Body Stats section
  3. Tap the edit icon
  4. Update your height, weight, age, or activity level
  5. Your BMI will automatically recalculate

Regular updates help CalNutriPal provide more accurate recommendations.

Is my data private?

Yes, your privacy is important to us. All personal and health data is stored securely and not shared with third parties. We use the data only to provide the services you expect from our app.

For complete details, please read our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Go to the Profile tab
  2. Scroll to Account section
  3. Tap "Reset App Data"
  4. Confirm your choice

This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.
